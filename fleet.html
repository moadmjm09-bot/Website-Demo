Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking 48–72 hours ahead for standard rides and 2–4 weeks ahead for weddings, proms, or large events. That said, our 24/7 dispatch can often accommodate same-day requests — just call.
Is gratuity included in the price?
A 20% gratuity and applicable fees are included in your quoted rate — no surprise charges or additional tipping expected, though always appreciated for exceptional service.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 24 hours or more before pickup receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a partial charge. Weddings and events follow a separate policy outlined in your contract.
Do you track flight delays?
Yes — every airport reservation includes complimentary live flight tracking, so your chauffeur adjusts automatically to early or delayed arrivals at no extra charge.
Can I request a specific chauffeur or vehicle?
Absolutely — note your preference in the booking form or mention it to our reservations team, and we'll do our best to accommodate, subject to availability.

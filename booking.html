How We Started

A Single Sedan, a Simple Standard

Denver Majestic Limousines was founded in 2009 on one idea: every ride, no matter how short, deserves the same care as a five-star hotel arrival. That standard hasn't moved in fifteen years, even as our fleet has grown from one sedan to six vehicle classes.

Today we're proud to be the preferred chauffeur service for Denver's leading hotels, wedding planners, and corporate travel desks — still family-operated, still hands-on with every reservation.
